General Emiliano Zapata

El Caudillo del Sur

Here is a list of awesome facts about General Zapata's life:

  • Emiliano Zapata Salazar was born on August 8, 1879 in Anenecuilco, Morelos.
  • According to historian Miguel León-Portilla, Zapata was fluent in Nahuatl.
  • The first military campaign of Zapata was the capture of the Hacienda of Chinameca.
  • Alongside Otilio Montaño Sánchez, promulgated the most radical reform plan in México: the Plan de Ayala.
  • Stablished the first comuna in Morelos, a people's oriented distribution of land and community centered harvest organization.

"El campesino tenía hambre, padecía miseria, sufría explotación y si se levantó en armas fue para obtener el pan que la avidez del rico le negaba."

-- Emiliano Zapata Salazar
